My Favourite Game

Super Mario

Saving the princess has been the most fascinating time of my childhood gaming years. Juming and killing enemies, earning coins by hitting bricks with your head and shooting bullets to take down the tortoises and other enemies were pretty exciting. Going from one level to another, jumping from one brick to another which have deep chasm between through impossible combination and after trying a lot for continuous days, testing my patience as a child, from going to nearly destroying my gaming console, all the nostalgic memory of my childhood and super mario are worth treasuring.
